Topos Theory (MGS 2022)

A topos is a category whose properties make it sufficiently similar to the category of sets than many of the constructions we make in set theory may also be carried out in an arbitrary topos. In addition to the geometric character, topoi share a strong connection with logic and type theory. In this course, we will examine the basics of the theory of topoi with an eye towards their applications in computer science. Possible topics include:
  • Presheaves and Sheaves
  • Grothendieck topologies
  • The subobject classifier
  • Lawvere-Tierney topologies
  • The internal language
  • Classifying topoi
